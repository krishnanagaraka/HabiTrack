Screenshot Guidelines:

  • Take screenshots on a real Android device or emulator
  • Use 16:9 aspect ratio (360x640 is good for phones)
  • Show actual app functionality, not mockups
  • Ensure text is readable and clear
  • Include 2-8 screenshots total
  • Save as PNG format
  • Name files: screenshot-1-dashboard.png, etc.
